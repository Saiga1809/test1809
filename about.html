ABOUT US



88 Organizer is a service-based company that was formed by Jacob Brooks in 2023. It was previously formed as 88 Nation which engaged in automotive sector namely 88 Showroom. 88 Nation went through a period of vacuum where the business they were running stopped for some quite time due to some business circumstances. During the idle time, Jacob held an open-discussion session with 88 Nation community about how to live the business up again. After a long discussion, they got an output which was to rebrand 88 Nation business to 88 Organizer and turn their main business to Event Organizer. The main business of 88 Organizer is Event Organizer, but it also acquisition 88 Nation’s previous business which is 88 Showroom. As the times goes by, the company keeps on innovating its product lines. Now the company is also expand its business sector in the photography, hospitality, and tourism sector. 88 Organizer’s vision mostly aiming on customer’s satisfaction as they were providing their best services to meets customer needs. The company always put its relationship with customers first as it strives for its growth together with their customers. Visit our main office at 101st Orion Road!
